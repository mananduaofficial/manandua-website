Wealth Management

Goal-aligned mutual fund portfolios across equity, debt, and select alternative categories, structured around your risk profile, time horizon, and evolving priorities.

What this service covers

Wealth management here is built around outcomes, not products. Every portfolio begins with a clear understanding of what you're working towards, how long you have, and the level of risk you're comfortable taking, before any investment is considered.

  • Goal-based portfolio construction: portfolios designed across equity, debt, and alternative categories, aligned to the specific requirements of each goal
  • SIP and lump sum strategy: a disciplined approach combining systematic investments with timely allocations where appropriate
  • Asset allocation framework: the right mix of assets based on your risk profile and time horizon, adjusted as your goals move closer
  • Ongoing portfolio review: periodic reviews and rebalancing to ensure your portfolio stays aligned as markets and life evolve

Regulatory & disclosure

Mutual fund schemes are distributed as an AMFI-registered Mutual Fund Distributor under a valid ARN (details available in the Investor Charter). Investments are offered under the Regular Plan option. Commission is received from Asset Management Companies for distribution, in accordance with the AMFI Code of Conduct.

Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks. Please read all scheme-related documents carefully before investing.
