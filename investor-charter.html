Investor Charter

For Mutual Fund Distributors · Last updated: [ADD DATE OF PUBLISHING]

This Investor Charter is published in line with SEBI's guidelines requiring registered intermediaries, including Mutual Fund Distributors (MFDs), to disclose their vision, services, investor rights, and grievance redressal mechanism in a clear and accessible format.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To help investors make informed, well-understood financial decisions, and to invest with knowledge and safety.

Mission: To provide clear, honest guidance and efficient service to investors in mutual fund distribution, while promoting financial literacy and protecting investor interests, in accordance with SEBI and AMFI regulations.

Details of Business Transacted

Manan Dua is an AMFI-registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN: [ADD YOUR ARN NUMBER], valid until [ADD VALIDITY DATE]), certified under NISM Series-V-A (Mutual Fund Distributors). In this capacity, the following business is transacted with investors:

  • Distribution of mutual fund schemes across multiple Asset Management Companies (AMCs), on a Regular Plan basis (as required for distributors under SEBI regulations)
  • Assistance with completion of Know Your Customer (KYC) formalities
  • Assistance with mutual fund transactions — purchases, redemptions, switches, and SIP set-up — on behalf of investors
  • Providing scheme-related information, account statements, and updates relevant to an investor's existing holdings

Commission is received from AMCs for schemes distributed, as disclosed under the AMFI Code of Conduct for Mutual Fund Distributors. Manan Dua does not deal in Direct Plans, in accordance with SEBI's regulatory framework for distributors.

Services Provided to Investors

  • Guidance on selecting mutual fund schemes appropriate to your stated financial goals, time horizon, and risk profile
  • Support with onboarding, KYC, and ongoing account servicing
  • Periodic portfolio reviews and updates on schemes you are invested in
  • Investor education through content shared via this website, newsletter, and social media channels

Rights of Investors

  • Right to receive services in a fair, transparent, and non-discriminatory manner
  • Right to know all commissions, charges, or fees applicable, and how they are received
  • Right to receive timely and accurate information about the mutual fund schemes you hold
  • Right to privacy and confidentiality of your personal and financial information
  • Right to a fair and timely grievance redressal process

Expectations from Investors

  • Completing KYC formalities accurately and keeping details updated
  • Reading scheme-related documents (SID, KIM) carefully before investing
  • Verifying the ARN of any mutual fund distributor before transacting
  • Providing accurate information about your financial goals, income, and risk appetite
  • Monitoring your investments periodically and raising concerns promptly, if any

Grievance Redressal Mechanism

If you have a complaint or grievance, it will be addressed through the following escalation steps:

  1. Step 1 — Direct contact: Write to us at contact@manandua.in with details of your complaint. We aim to acknowledge your complaint promptly and work towards resolution directly, or in coordination with the concerned AMC where relevant.
  2. Step 2 — SEBI SCORES: If your complaint is not resolved to your satisfaction, you may lodge it with SEBI through the SCORES portal (SEBI Complaints Redress System) at scores.sebi.gov.in.
  3. Step 3 — Online Dispute Resolution (ODR): For unresolved disputes, you may also access the SEBI-mandated Online Dispute Resolution mechanism through the Smart ODR portal at smartodr.in, which offers online conciliation and arbitration.

Dos and Don'ts for Investors

Do

  • Deal only with SEBI-registered intermediaries and AMFI-registered distributors; always verify the ARN
  • Read all scheme-related documents before investing
  • Keep your KYC details updated and monitor your folio statements regularly
  • Ask questions about commissions, charges, and risks before investing

Don't

  • Don't invest based on unsolicited tips, assured or guaranteed return promises, or social media hype
  • Don't share your login credentials, OTPs, or transaction passwords with anyone, including your distributor
  • Don't make payments to a distributor's personal account — all mutual fund payments should be made directly in favour of the scheme or AMC
  • Don't hesitate to escalate a grievance if it isn't resolved satisfactorily at the first level
