About Manan Dua

Bridging financial wisdom with practical clarity.

I believe financial literacy is one of the most powerful tools for creating freedom and peace of mind. This work is about sharing practical insights, meaningful conversations, and connecting with people who want to build sustainable wealth and make informed financial decisions.

Over the years, I've worked with business owners, senior professionals, NRIs, and young families who had built significant wealth, but lacked clarity, structure, and a trusted partner to guide them. That's where I come in.
